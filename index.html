Goshen Springs

Promoting education among marginalized communities
Goshen Springs is an institution created by Moses Buli, with help from donors, to help a total of 108 pupils from the local community get proper basic education. We believe that education is key to the success of our pupils. We believe that education plays an important role in empowering our kids to think critically, make discrete decisions and become the leaders of tomorrow.

Pupils during assembly

Due to a financial hurdle, the Headmaster of Joy Geomar Primary school, the only primary school at Impiron, decided to discontinue the school (2019). The closure of Joy Geomar led to the displacement of 150 pupils who could no longer attend school. Moses Buli, the sole proprietor of Kilimanjaro Resort, decided to start Goshen Springs, a new primary school created to accommodate the displaced students. Goshen Springs is currently under development and we are currently seeking all the help that we could possibly find in order to fully develop the school.

Board Games

By forming a Chess Club and Checkers Club, we hope to help our pupils discover new hobbies and interests, as well as jog their memory and improve their learning ability.

Library

We are excited to build a well equipped library and launch a book club. We have already reached out to bookstores requesting books on diverse topics. The book club is intended to help pupils improve their reading skills and nurture their reading ability at an early age.

Computer Library

With help from donor funding, we hope to build a well equipped computer lab in order to acquaint pupils with basic computer skills before transitioning to high school.

Outdoors Club

We look forward to launching a soccer club and a basketball club to enable our pupils to engage in sports outside class. With help from donors, we hope to acquire the sports equipment and gaming kits required in order to fully implement the project.

Get in touch with Moses Buli to learn more about our work and how you can get involved. We will be excited to receive any kind of help from you.
